Explore Your Potential

Connect, learn, and explore through impactful, short-term projects. At Portlab, everyone has the opportunity to work on real projects with real clients.

What can I find at Portlab?

Throughout Fall 2022, we will be primarily focusing on data science projects. According to your preferences, you will be paired with groups of 3-6 students to work on client-based projects that span from 4-6 weeks. The scope and complexity vary by project, but teams may use data visualizations, statistical inference, and machine learning, to name a few, to address clients' problem statements. Our partnering clients range from on-campus units to local startups and businesses. While we are still devising and finalizing the materials, you can expect resources such as tutorials, workshops, and office hours to help prepare you and guide you through the projects.

Who can join Portlab?

Literally anyone! We pride ourselves on our inclusiveness, and as long as you are willing to commit and complete a few prerequisites (e.g., training materials, informational interviews), you will be eligible to participate in Portlab projects anytime, anywhere.

Why should I join Portlab?

If any of these descriptions below resonate with you, Portlab will be just the place for you:
  • Oh geez! I have no idea if I'll enjoy doing what I'm learning in school right now after I graduate...
  • I'm dying to put my skills to the test and see what my desired role truly entails!
  • I'm screwed... I'm applying for internships and jobs but I have no actual projects to show for...
  • I aspire to use real data to solve real problems and leave an impact on the local community!
  • My schedule is packed, but I always love working with my peers and learning a thing or two from them!
  • I wonder what tools and procedures are used to manage projects in actual work settings!

In short, if you are looking for a no-frills project-based learning experience, you won't be disappointed:) Check out our Festifall flyer for a TL;DR!

How much time will I need to commit?

You can choose to join whatever number of projects you wish to and whenever you desire! We will try our best to keep them coming:) Generally speaking, each project will last for 4-6 weeks and take up 4-6 hours per week per person. We will be holding occasional workshops and office hours, but nothing is mandatory! Participation in these additional events will not affect your chances of being matched with projects.
